# Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy: Auditly.ai

## Phase 1: Community Seed (Days 3-4)
- **Peerlist & IndieHackers:** Launch as a "Product in Progress." In the 2026 market, users love "Build in Public" transparency.
- **Developer DAO Groups:** Share the audit logic in Bengaluru-based Discord/WhatsApp developer groups to get feedback on the pricing benchmarks.

## Phase 2: The "Savings Screenshot" (Virality)
- **Feature:** Implement a "Share on X" button that generates a pre-filled tweet: 
  > "I just found ₹23,988 in hidden AI spend leaks using Auditly.ai! 🛡️ Check your stack: [Link]"
- **Goal:** Use "Proof of Savings" as a social currency to drive organic growth.

## Phase 3: The B2B Pivot (Scaling)
- **Target:** Small Indian SaaS startups (5-20 employees).
- **Offer:** A "Team Audit" report that identifies "Zombie Seats" in Cursor/ChatGPT Team plans, saving companies ₹10k+ per month instantly.
