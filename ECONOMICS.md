## The "Redundancy Matrix"
Auditly AI identifies three primary "Leakage" patterns in the Indian market:

1. **The IDE Overlap (High Severity):** Users paying for Cursor (₹1,680) + Copilot (₹840). 
   * *Saving:* **₹840/mo**.
2. **The LLM Drift (Medium Severity):** Users paying for ChatGPT Plus (₹1,999) while also having Claude Pro (₹1,680).
   * *Saving:* **₹1,999/mo** (by utilizing ChatGPT Go for free tasks).
3. **The Zombie Seat (Low Severity):** Unused seats in Cursor Business tiers.
   * *Saving:* **₹3,360/seat/mo**.

For an early-stage Indian startup with 5 developers, optimizing this stack can reclaim over **₹1.2 Lakhs annually**—extending their technical runway by approximately 4–6 weeks of cloud infrastructure costs.
